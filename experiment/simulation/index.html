PROCEDURE

Basic Knowledge
  1. Answer all the questions and click on "Submit Test" button.
  2. Click on next level button which will appear in place of submit button below the test.
Configuration
  1. Select Configuration Type, Material type, Sensor type, and Voltage.
  2. Click on "CHECK CONFIGURATION" button.
Mimic
  1. Click on red circle available near terminal label and connect to positive voltage, negative voltage, and ground. Connect all terminals one by one as shown in above circuit diagram of NPN type configuration or PNP type configuration.
  2. Click on "Check Connection Status" button.
  3. Click on "NEXT LEVEL" button.
Animation
  1. Select Material type and sensor type for animation of proximity sensor.
  2. Click on "SUBMIT" button.
  3. Follow Step6 and Step7 for sensing all material types or targets by Proximity Sensor.
  4. After checking all materials or targets, click on "SELECT ANOTHER CONFIGURATION TYPE".
  5. After all steps, click on "FINISH" button.
  • NOTE : Follow same steps of "Configuration", "Mimic" and "Animation" for another type of configuration.
